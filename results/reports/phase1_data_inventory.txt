PHASE 1 DATA INVENTORY — Zenodo 57911 (Rishikesh Meditation Study)
=====================================================================
Source: Braboszcz et al. (2017) "Increased Gamma Brainwave Amplitude
        Compared to Control in Three Different Meditation Traditions"
DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.57911

GROUPS (n=16 per group):
  CTR  = Control (novice, 0 hours experience)
  ISY  = Isha Shoonya (open awareness, ~2625 hours)
  VIP  = Vipassana (focused attention, ~9201 hours)
  HYT  = Himalayan Yoga Tradition (~15475 hours)

CONDITIONS:
  MW   = Mind Wandering (baseline)
  MED  = Meditation
  BRE  = Breathing (gamma CSV only)

FILES:
------
1. boxplot_alpha.csv (787 bytes)
   Shape: 64 rows x 2 columns
   Columns: Groupe, Median Alpha
   Content: Per-subject median alpha power (7-11 Hz), MW+MED combined
   Groups: CTR(16), ISY(16), VIP(16), HYT(16)

2. boxplot_gamma.csv (2016 bytes)
   Shape: 64 rows x 4 columns
   Columns: Groupe, Condition, Electrodes, Median.Gamma
   Content: Per-subject median gamma (60-110 Hz), parieto-occipital, MW+MED
   Groups: CTR(16), ISY(16), VIP(16), HYT(16)

3. median_alpha_power.csv (3137 bytes)
   Shape: 128 rows x 4 columns
   Columns: Subjects, Group, Condition, Alpha
   Content: Per-subject median alpha power, MW and MED conditions separate
   Groups x Conditions: 4 x 2 = 8 cells, 16 subjects each

4. median_gamma_power.csv (10353 bytes)
   Shape: 192 rows x 9 columns
   Columns: Subjects, Group, Condition, Gamma_All, Posterior, Frontal,
            Combined_Post, Combined_Front, Combined_all
   Content: Per-subject gamma power with regional breakdown
   Conditions: MW, MED, BRE (3 conditions x 4 groups x 16 subjects)

5. 711Hz_spec_data_medit.mat (18001 bytes)
   Variables:
     CTR_medit_711: shape (64, 16) — 64 channels x 16 subjects
     ISY_medit_711: shape (64, 16)
     VIP_medit_711: shape (64, 16)
     HYT_medit_711: shape (64, 16)
   Content: Channel-level alpha spectral power during MED condition

6. 60110Hz_spec_data_medit.mat (21549 bytes)
   Variables:
     CTR_medit60110: shape (64, 16) — 64 channels x 16 subjects
     ISY_medit60110: shape (64, 16)
     VIP_medit60110: shape (64, 16)
     HYT_medit60110: shape (64, 16)
   Content: Channel-level gamma spectral power during MED condition

7. chanlocs.mat (5504 bytes)
   Variable: chanlocs, shape (1, 77)
   Fields: labels, type, theta, radius, X, Y, Z, sph_theta, sph_phi,
           sph_radius, urchan, ref
   Content: 77 EEG electrode locations (64 used in spectral data)

WHAT IS AVAILABLE vs NOT AVAILABLE:
  Available: Group-level summary statistics (median power per subject)
  Available: Channel-level spectral power (64 ch x 16 subj per group)
  NOT available: Raw EEG time series (needed for KWW, Phase 2)
  NOT available: Time-resolved spectral evolution within session
  NOT available: Phase information (needed for Berry phase test)
  NOT available: ICA components
