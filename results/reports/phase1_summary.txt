PHASE 1 SUMMARY — Rishikesh Meditation Study (Zenodo 57911)
=============================================================
Date: 2026-03-08
Dataset: Braboszcz et al. (2017), 4 groups x 16 subjects
         CTR (0h), ISY/Shoonya (2625h), VIP/Vipassana (9201h), HYT (15475h)

WHAT PHASE 1 SHOWED:
---------------------
1. HYT alpha/gamma power ratio = 1.361, within 2.1% of 4/3 (1.333)
   - This is the CLOSEST ratio to any Merkabit constant in the dataset
   - ISY (Shoonya) alpha/gamma = 1.428, within 7.1% of 4/3
   - Both open-awareness-like traditions approach 4/3

2. Alpha power does NOT monotonically increase with experience
   - VIP has highest alpha (48.7 dB, p < 0.01 vs all others)
   - HYT has LOWEST alpha (42.3 dB) — below controls (44.3)
   - This dissociates experience from simple alpha enhancement

3. Gamma power shows near-monotonic increase with experience
   - CTR(28.0) < ISY(30.9) ~ VIP(30.5) ~ HYT(31.0)
   - All meditator groups elevated above controls

4. HYT signature: LOW alpha + HIGH gamma = ratio near 4/3
   - Distinct from VIP: HIGH alpha + HIGH gamma = ratio far from 4/3
   - Consistent with HYT achieving cooperative spectral balance
     rather than driven alpha enhancement

5. No significant variance differences between groups (Levene p=0.81)
   - But this tests BETWEEN-subject variance of session medians
   - Cannot test WITHIN-session temporal variance without raw EEG

6. Posterior/frontal gamma ratio ~ 1.00 for all groups
   - No anterior-posterior gradient differences
   - Gamma enhancement is spatially uniform

7. MW vs MED difference is small and non-significant for all groups
   - Alpha power nearly identical between conditions
   - This is consistent with "trait" rather than "state" effects

WHAT PHASE 1 CANNOT TEST:
--------------------------
- KWW alpha_KWW exponent (requires raw EEG time series)
- |C| convergence to 1/3 (requires Hilbert envelope of raw signal)
- Z3 symmetry (requires inter-channel phase from raw EEG)
- Berry phase cancellation (requires instantaneous frequency)
- Within-session temporal variance of alpha_KWW
- Comparison with CHB-MIT pre-ictal trajectory

-----------------
The Phase 1 spectral data provides one clear signal:
  HYT alpha/gamma = 1.361 ~ 4/3

This is a NECESSARY but not SUFFICIENT condition for the Merkabit
framework. The alpha/gamma ratio tells us the SPECTRAL BALANCE
approaches the predicted value, but does not tell us about:
